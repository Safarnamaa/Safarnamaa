About Us

Safarnama-The alumni meet of Electrical Engineering Depertment from Netaji Subhash Engineering College, one of the awated and largest festival. Safarnama gives you a chance to come back to your second home, that clime, from where it all started, where you have spent four magical years of your life. It's time to dive into the sea of nostalgia. Safarnama combines culture, fun, nostalgia, food, knowledge all alone. The platform gives you a chance to showcase your hidden tallent ❤️🤍.
